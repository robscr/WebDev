HOW TO PLAY CHESSLESHIP!

Try to uncover all the hidden pieces with the least amount of guesses.

Select up to 8 Squares with mouse/touch and press guess to uncover.
(This is 1 turn, but be careful you only have 8)

Left click or press the "Flag" button to flag for a piece.
(keep clcking to cycle through or unflag)


Yellow Squares- Targeted by 1 or more pieces
The number in the Yellow Cells indicates how many STILL HIDDEN pieces target that square

Red Squares- You've Found a Piece!
The letter in the square tells you which one

Black Squares- Uh oh! No piece is targetting this square
